SVN IMproved plugin

*sd* svn diff. You can also get the diff of version 123 by pressing *123sd*.

*ss* svn status. When the status windows is open you can press *x* to choose
wherever file you want to commit and then *c* in order to commit.

*su* svn update.

*sl* svn log. By default will show last 10 commits, you can press *20sl* to 
get last 20 commits.
